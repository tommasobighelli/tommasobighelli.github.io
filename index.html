Tommaso Bighelli




Welcome! I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the Halle Institute for Economic Research (IWH) .

I am also guest researcher at Deutsche Bundesbank and researcher in the Scientific Team of the Competitiveness Research Network (CompNet) .

Previous experiences include a research visit at Bank of England (summer 2022), a Research Master from Autonomous University of Barcelona, a MSc and a BSc from Sapienza University of Rome and several collaborations as research assistant.



Main Interests: Firm Dynamics, Structural Change, Productivity, Factors reallocation, Corporate Taxation, Market structure
