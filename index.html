Tommaso Bighelli




Welcome! I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the Halle Institute for Economic Research - IWH where I work under the supervision of Javier Miranda and Matthias Mertens.

In my research I combine empirical methods to investigate on Firm Dynamics, International Trade and Firm Productivity.

I also work as researcher in the Scientific Team of the Competitiveness Research Network (CompNet) .

Before my PhD, I obtained a MRes in Economic Analysis from Autonomous University of Barcelona, a MSc and a BSc in Economics from Sapienza University of Rome
